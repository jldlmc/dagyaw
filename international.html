Did you know that UNICEF is dedicated to promoting and protecting the rights of every Filipino child? They focus especially on those who are most vulnerable and disadvantaged.

Your support can make a real difference! Every donation helps UNICEF continue their vital work and reach more children in need. Join us in making a positive impact on the future of Filipino children.
