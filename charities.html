Kanlungan ni Maria

Home for the Aged, Inc.

Kanlungan ni Maria is a loving home for the poor, abandoned, neglected, homeless, and sick elderly. Here, we truly live out Christ's values, with Mary as our Mother and Model.

Our residents are treated with dignity and receive the love, affection, and support they deserve from our dedicated staff and volunteers. For those whose families cannot be found, we work hard to find long-term homes.
